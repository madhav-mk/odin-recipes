Oven BBQ Beef Brisket

Delicious restaurant-quality brisket, in the comfort of your own home!

BBQ brisket is great for feeding a family. This easy recipe only has 3 ingredients and requires very little attention as it slow cooks for hours in the oven until the brisket is tender and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds beef brisket
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • Other ingredients

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 160C.
  2. Coat brisket with seasoning.
  3. Place in the oven and cook for 3 hours.
  4. Remove and let it rest for 30 minutes.
