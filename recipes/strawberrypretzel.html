Judy's Strawberry Pretzel Salad

This three-layer strawberry pretzel dessert salad includes a pretzel crust, cream cheese center, and strawberry top.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup crushed pretzels
  • 3/4 cup butter, melted
  • 4 1/2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • Other ingredients

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients, preheat oven to 350C.
  2. Mix dry ingredients then wet ingredients.
  3. Put in a tray by layers then bake for 30 minutes.
